Roast Lamb with Gravy

Home

A perfect roast leg of lamb with a delicious gravy to match

The best recipe for a succulent roast leg of lamb with potatoes, carrots, peas and gravy

Ingredients

  • 3kg leg of lamb
  • 1tsp cooking salt
  • 1/2tsp pepper
  • 1 tbsp fresh rosemary leaves
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 whole heads of garlic (halved)
  • Few sprigs of rosemary
  • 4 tbsp flour (for the gravy)
  • 2 1/2 cups of beef broth or stock (for the gravy)

Steps

  1. Take lamb out of fridge 1 hour before roasting
  2. Preheat oven to 240 degrees with oven shelf in the middle
  3. Prepare rub by mixing rosemary, olive oil and garlic together
  4. Place lamb in roasting pan and coat with garlic rosemary rub prepared in previous step
  5. Add salt and pepper to lamb to taste
  6. Roast lamb leg for 20mins then reduce temperature to 180 degrees and roast for 1 hour until internal temperature is 53 degrees (checking after 45 mins)
  7. Remove lamb from oven,cover loosely with tin foil and let it rest for 20mins
  8. Serve and enjoy with gravy
