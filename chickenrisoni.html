One-pot Greek Chicken Risoni

Home

An easy recipe to will have a healthy meal in front of you ready to demolish in no time

Ingredients

  • 2 tsps olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 red capsicum
  • 2 zucchini
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 cups risoni
  • 2 cups of cherry tomatoes
  • Add salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Marinade Chicken
  2. Cook Chicken
  3. Preheat oven
  4. Saute vegetables
  5. Add everything together
  6. Scatter chicken
  7. Bake for 15 minutes
