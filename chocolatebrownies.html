Flourless Chocolate Brownies

Home

A quick easy recipe for chocolate brownies with the added bonus of them being gluten free

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 85g unsalted butter
  • 1/4 tsp cooking salt
  • 2/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2/3 cup almond meal
  • 1/3 cup rice flour

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees
  2. Grease and square pan
  3. Melt butter and chocolate together
  4. Cool for 5 minutes
  5. Add vanilla, sugar and eggs
  6. Add dry ingredients to the wet
  7. Bake in oven for 25 minutes
  8. Cool and serve
